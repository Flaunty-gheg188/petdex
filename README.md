## 🚀 Using Petdex

The interface stays simple. When you open the application, you see your gallery dashboard. 

* **The Main View:** Use your mouse to scroll through your list of pets. Click any pet to expand the animation.
* **Filtering:** Use the search bar at the top to find specific pets. You can also click the filters button to hide pets you do not own.
* **Settings:** Access the gear icon in the bottom corner to change the theme. You can switch between light and dark modes to suit your preference.
* **Saving:** Any changes you make to your sort order save automatically. You do not need to click a manual save button.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the software does not work, check these common fixes:

* **File blocked by Windows:** Windows may show a blue box saying the PC is protected. Click More Info and then Run Anyway to proceed.
* **White screen on startup:** Ensure your graphics drivers stay updated. Visit your computer manufacturer website if the problem persists.
* **Missing pets:** Click the Refresh button in the top menu to re-sync your collection from the cloud.
* **Slow animations:** Close other heavy programs like video editors while you run the gallery to free up your computer memory.
